How It Works

Our Business Workflow

A seamless, transparent process from first contact to your doorstep — designed for maximum efficiency and zero friction at every step.

01

Enquiry & Registration

Reach us via our app, website, or walk into our store. Businesses register as wholesale partners — individuals can order directly. No complex onboarding.

02

Browse & Select

Explore 10,000+ products on the app or in-store. Filter by category, brand, price, or availability. Add to cart — single items or full pallets, your choice.

03

Place Your Order

Confirm your cart and select your delivery slot. Wholesale partners can choose scheduled bulk delivery. Retail and app customers pick same-day or next-day delivery.

04

Warehouse Fulfilment

Our warehouse team picks, packs, and quality-checks your order. Cold-chain products are handled separately. Bulk orders are palletised and documented for your records.

05

Live Tracking

Track your delivery in real time through the VMM Traders app. Receive ETA updates, driver contact details, and delivery confirmation notifications — full transparency.

06

Delivered & Invoiced

Products delivered to your door. A digital GST invoice is instantly available in your app or email. Rate your experience — your feedback shapes how we improve.
